# Video Quality Scorecard

Score 1-5 for each dimension.

| Dimension | 1 | 3 | 5 |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Hook clarity | Confusing first second | Understandable but weak | Instantly clear and scroll-stopping |
| Subject consistency | Object/character changes | Minor drift | Stable anchor throughout |
| Camera fidelity | Ignores requested camera | Partly follows | Matches framing and movement |
| Motion readability | Chaotic or too busy | Mostly readable | Clean, timed, and natural |
| Platform fit | Wrong format | Usable with edits | Native to the target platform |
